Post Hospitalization Rehab

Long duration hospitalization for surgery, serious illness or any other reason could be a life-changing experience. Physical and mental state of the patient gets a set back and it could take good deal of time and effort to adapt to the changes. At Dr. Bharathi Rao’s, our rehabilitation programs are designed and personalized as per the patient’s unique requirement to help them get back to their pre-hospitalization days and experience convenience and normalcy.

“Taking you back to normalcy after hospitalization is our commitment.”

Why you need this rehabilitation program?

Our rehabilitation programs guided by experts include one-on-one care as well as group support sessions to help a patient recover physically and mentally.

  • It helps your mind and body cope with the changes and recover faster.

  • It helps reclaim your abilities in a step-by-step manner like walking, going to toilet, dressing up, climbing stairs and so on.

  • Support and guidance to become more independent.

  • To get complete rest, medical and nursing care in the initial days.

  • Group activities and sessions to help you reveal your inner fear and win over them.

How this rehabilitation will help you recover?

Long hospitalizations require your patience, time and effort for getting back to normalcy. Here are a few ways this program is going to help you.

  • It helps in pain management and pain reduction in the designated area of surgery.

  • It will facilitate increased range of functionality at the site of surgery or procedure

  • It will strengthen the muscles and raise endurance

  • Gradually, you will glide back to the pre-operative basic life activities

The use of state-of-the art medical devices and equipment facilitate smooth physical activity and transition of the patients towards normalcy. Vast spread of greenery further offers a conductive and positive environment for better engagement of the patients in their self-care.

Rest assured that you are not alone in your journey! Our trained staffs and experienced professionals will walk along with you to help you glide through this change with a lot of confidence and convenience. To know more, send us an enquiry

Palliative Care

Rare diseases like multiple sclerosis, muscular dystrophy, myasthenia gravis, osteogenesis imperfect, fibrous dysplasia or McCune-Albright syndrome and X-linked hypophosphatemia along with chronic neurological disorders, chronic cardiopulmonary conditions, and mental illnesses make the day-to-day lives of patients challenging. They are not only physically painful and restrictive but also damaging to the psyche.

Palliative care helps such patients and their families overcome the associated stress, regain positivity and make the daily living easy and more of a fulfilling experience. We, at Dr. Bharathi Rao’s, design comprehensive rehabilitation programs catering every aspect of the patient care for better living experience, satisfaction, and hope.

We design a holistic rehabilitation program for severely ill patients keeping their individual needs in mind for making their everyday living more independent with peaceful state of mind and relief from pain and suffering.

Medical and nursing care:

Complete administration and monitoring of the patient’s treatment regimen by expert physicians followed by appropriate nursing care offered in a personalized manner.

Comprehensive rehabilitation/therapeutic intervention:

1. Physiotherapy
2. Occupational therapy
3. Psychiatric consultation
4. Respiratory therapy
5. Speech therapy
6. And other required therapeutic services

Comprehensive diagnostic services

1. Medical diagnostic services (x-ray, blood investigation and more)
2. Rehab diagnostic services (audiological and visual services and more)

Emergency medical care

1. Emergency critical care unit
2. Ambulance service

Nutrition

A well-planned diet and nutrition catering to specific needs of the patients.

Hospice Care:

Prioritizing care and quality of life, our team assists the terminally ill patients attain their physical, emotional, and spiritual needs while reducing pain and suffering.

“Live with dignity! Move towards hope!”

Why you need this rehabilitation program?

  • Builds patient’s self-confidence, protects the dignity, and raises the hope.

  • Ensures complete physical, mental, social, and spiritual wellbeing.

  • Develops support system for the patient as well as the family.

  • Improves communication between the patient, caregivers and the healthcare providers.

  • Helps discover pathways for future treatment and recovery.

Program Highlights

  • Personalized care because every patient is unique

  • Well-designed program for every age groups

  • Activity-based and recreational routine designed for the patients for maximum engagement.
