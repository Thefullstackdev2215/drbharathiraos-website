Mission

To ensure highest level of patient recovery and maximum
participation of people in life with excellence in
rehabilitation services.

Vision

To foster human dignity by encouraging a self-reliant life
among people with illnesses, injuries or disabilities
through standard rehabilitation practices that restore
physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual wellbeing.

Our Values / Our Guiding Principles

Respect for all: We maintain the decency in human
behavior by showing respect to every patient, their
families, and our co-workers.
Patient-Centric: The patients come first in
everything we do. We prioritize patient’s needs and
personalize care for them for the best outcomes.
Learning and innovation: We learn each day and from every person to continuously improve and excel in the services we offer and adopt latest innovations to maximize efficiency in service delivery.
Compassion: We keep compassion at the core while serving patients and building hopes among the families to enhance their experience.

About the Group

Dr. Bharti Rao Advanced Comprehensive Rehabilitation Center is one of the flagship initiatives by St. Mary’s
Group which already enjoys a magnanimous presence in the world of education and other businesses across
different industry verticals. The group runs several engineering colleges with huge campus, academic
amenities, and residential facilities across Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, and West Bengal with
plans for further expansion pan India. Over decades of its unrivaled existence, St. Mary’s has become a
landmark for many who entrust their career and future to the organization. Over the years, it has carved its
name in the niche of science, technology, research consulting and sports contributing to the growth of
communities and the nation.
The founding team and the management operate with a clear purpose of bringing palpable difference in
delivering service to the end users while abiding by the values of compassion, integrity, and transparency.
The result is a humungous tribe of students across the nation who identify themselves with the organization.
Dr. Bharti Rao’s Rehabilitation Services is all geared up to add another feather to the cap of parent group
organization with its unmatched facilities, aesthetically designed vast campus in the greenery, and an all-
inclusive treatment facility offered with highest standard of professionalism and human compassion.

CMD Message

Dear All,
We had been in the business of serving people and touching lives since decades. Now, with Dr. Bharti Rao’s Rehabilitation initiative, we are making our tangible presence in the noble cause of bringing health, happiness, and hope. Our efforts are meaningful only if we succeed in this cause and offer a comforting experience to the people who long to live a normal life with good health, independence and respect.
This rehabilitation center is not only a brick-and-mortar elevation, but our vision for creating a world of difference for people whose lives matter and whose unique health challenges are seen with respect. Our facility, equipped with the best-in-class infrastructure, equipment, and devices and supported by chosen doctors, therapists, healthcare professionals, and staffs, would surely create an atmosphere of healing for the patients, people with disabilities, illnesses, and congenital disorders at a cost that can be easily afforded.

This journey towards wellbeing of people and their families is our responsibility but a call to everyone to join hands, make commitments and march forward for a triumph.
Let’s make it happen! Let’s bring the change!
